Each card shows the piles and the rule of that version.

Game 1 — Misère Nim

Piles: 1, 3, 5, 7

Rules: On each turn, a player removes stones from only one pile. The player who takes the last stone loses.

Game 2 — Nim (Forbidden First Pile)

Piles: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5

Rules: On each turn, a player removes stones from only one pile. The first player to take a stone from the first pile loses.

Game 3 — Classic Nim

Piles: One pile of 17

Rules: On each turn, a player may remove 1, 2, or 3 stones. The player who takes the last stone loses.
